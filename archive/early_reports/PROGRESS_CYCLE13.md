## THE ENCODING SYSTEM (best current model)

The Voynich uses a SYSTEMATIC PREFIX-SUFFIX encoding:
- Prefix determines CATEGORY (ch-=above-ground, sh-=below-ground, etc.)
- Suffix determines GRAMMATICAL FORM (-ol=base, -or=variant, -ey=another form)
- Function words (daiin, ol, or, ar, al) connect plant-part words
- The text describes plants PART BY PART, matching the illustrations
